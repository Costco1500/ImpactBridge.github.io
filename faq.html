Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up as a volunteer?

Visit the Volunteer Opportunities section, select an area of interest, and follow the instructions to apply.

What are the age requirements for volunteering?

Most opportunities are available for volunteers aged 16 and above. Specific opportunities may have different age requirements, so please check individual listings for details.

What should I expect during my first volunteer experience?

During your first experience, you'll be introduced to the team, given an orientation, and briefed on your responsibilities.
